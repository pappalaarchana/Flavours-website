Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers that might help you:

  • How to place an order?
    To place an order, simply browse our menu, select your favorite items, and click "Add to Cart." Once you've added all your items, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, and confirm your order.
  • Can I modify my order after placing it?
    Unfortunately, orders cannot be modified once they are confirmed. Please review your order carefully before completing the checkout process.
  • How can I track my order?
    Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can use this link to monitor the status and location of your delivery in real-time.

Live Chat Support
